About us

Sinergo elektronika was established in 2007 and is a privately held, small-sized company operating in the field of electrical installations and telecommunications. After years of experience in various electrical installation projects, we got the chance to expand our business to telecommunications. Step-by-step we grew in know-how and established ourselves on the Austrian and German markets. In 2018 we came to a revenue of 0,5 mil. EUR and count more than 20 high-skilled employees.

Our Vision and Mission

To build a company with a strong presence in telecommunication services. A long-term „win-win“ cooperation with our clients. To provide our clients with full service at a professional level and within agreed deadlines.

Our Services

Sinergo elektronika provides telecommunication companies with the service of installation, replacement and maintenance of telecommunication equipment. We are also able to provide electrical installation services during the construction of new and reconstruction of existing facilities. Throughout many years of working alongside Austrian and German companies, we have gained extensive experience and knowledge in the implementation of complex telecommunication and electrical installation projects, which makes us a long-term and reliable partner. Flexibility, speed, competence and the possibility of quick engagement of external experts are the main advantages of Sinergo. We are always ready to provide our clients with professional and qualified teams in the shortest possible time.

2007

Establishment of the company with headquarters in Zagreb, Croatia. In cooperation with Končar d.d. we started working on several electrical installation projects as well maintenance of HEP’s hydropower plants and substations in Croatia.

2014

First engagement on dismantling of A1 base stations in Austria.
Preparation for the Tele2 Croatia 4G and GPON projects, which we were part of in the following two years.

2017

Veldman Group - renovation of electrical installations, energetics and Inhouse installation in Lower Saxony, Germany.

2018

Started working on the GU7 Consolidation Project of Telefónica Germany. Up to this day we have dismantled equipment on 500+ sites and remain working on the project.

2019

Started working on the Andromeda project (Deutsche Telekom) in Bayern, Germany. Later that year we also engaged our teams on the Andromeda project in Croatia (Hrvatski Telekom) .

2020

Andromeda Refarming project, preparation of radio equipment for 5G. GL900/1800 project for Telefónica Germany (Nokia AirScale) .
