2007

Establishment of the company with headquarters in Zagreb, Croatia. In cooperation with Končar d.d. we started working on several electrical installation projects as well maintenance of HEP’s hydropower plants and substations in Croatia.

2014

First engagement on dismantling of A1 base stations in Austria.
Preparation for the Tele2 Croatia 4G and GPON projects, which we were part of in the following two years.

2017

Veldman Group - renovation of electrical installations, energetics and Inhouse installation in Lower Saxony, Germany.

2018

Started working on the GU7 Consolidation Project of Telefónica Germany. Up to this day we have dismantled equipment on 500+ sites and remain working on the project.

2019

Started working on the Andromeda project (Deutsche Telekom) in Bayern, Germany. Later that year we also engaged our teams on the Andromeda project in Croatia (Hrvatski Telekom) .

2020

Andromeda Refarming project, preparation of radio equipment for 5G. GL900/1800 project for Telefónica Germany (Nokia AirScale) .
